I was shortlisted for the Specialist Programmer role at Infosys through campus placements. The process involved an initial online assessment covering Data Structures, Algorithms, and Problem Solving, followed by two technical interviews and one HR round.

During the technical interviews, I was asked questions related to core DSA concepts, system design fundamentals, and my past projects. I made sure to clearly explain the logic and real-life use cases of my code. I also highlighted my MERN stack shopping website and how I integrated Razorpay for payments.

In the HR round, I was asked about my motivations, future goals, and how I balance academics and projects. I shared my passion for building impactful tech and contributing to global solutions.

Finally, I received the offer for the Specialist Programmer role with a CTC of 9.5 LPA. This experience strengthened my confidence and reaffirmed my love for backend and full-stack development.

Tips: Consistent DSA practice, building real projects, and clarity in communication helped me crack the role. Stay confident and keep learning!
